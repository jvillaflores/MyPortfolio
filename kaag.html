KAAG: A Mobile-based Multimodal Preservation for Kagan Language and Culture.

KAAG is a capstone thesis project we made as 4 member group. KAAG was made for Kagan Language and Culture preservation. It was classified that Kagan Language is endangered which is why our group focused on this tribe and did a research.

Contribution

I was the front-end developer of KAAG. I followed a UI/UX Design that was designed by our UI/UX Designer and used React Native Expo documentation to design KAAG. I also designed the logo of KAAG, the color was based from the color scheme that was planned by the designer. This was the first time I used React Native framework but I am have used HTML, CSS, JavaScript before.

Description

Language Preservation Application with learning materials, dictionary, upload images and texts, contribute a word and meaning, audio for dicitonary, and be a Kagan Validator.

Features

  • Learning Materials
  • Dictionary
  • Social Feed
  • Contribution
  • Validator
