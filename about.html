Jan Millena J. Villaflores

Graphic Designer | Digital Marketing

As an experienced designer for 7+ years, professionally working for 1 yr and 9 months, and as a student freelance designer for 6+ years with a diverse skill set. Currently, I work as a Digital Marketing Manager, while also taking freelance video and graphic design editing. I oversee marketing collaterals, email marketing, and social media reports ensuring a high-quality results for our clients. As Digital Designer, I create landing pages, social media banners, and code EDMs. My passion for design started in secondary school, and I have since honed my skills through part-time and freelance commissions. Designing comes naturally to me, and I am constantly eager to discover new design styles.

Personality-wise, as an INFJ, I have excellent listening and information-gathering abilities, which contribute to my success as a designer. I thrive in both independent and collaborative work environments, particularly when working with like-minded individuals who value teamwork.

Experienced in using Swipepages as a website builder, Photoshop, Canva, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro, Adobe XD for banners, posters, and other graphic design contents. I also have technical skills which include React Native Expo, HTML5, JavaScript, CSS.
