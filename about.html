Jan Millena J. Villaflores

UX/UI Designer, Front-end Developer, Graphic Designer

I am Jan Millena J. Villaflores, from Davao City, Philippines. I graduated Bachelor of Science in Information Technology specialized in Learning Technologies - CumLaude at the University of the Immaculate Conception, Davao City. I joined college offices, as a Secretary of our College Department. I gathered my experiences from the projects and commissions I accepted while I am stil a student. I have designed logos, posters, pubmats for social media. While studying, I have learned about programming and realized that my skills in programming excels more on the front-end. My passion in design started since I was in High School, designed tshirts for our club and class shirts, designed movie posters for school requirements. I also gained experiences from part time jobs that would require to design based on what the client wants. I would like to explore more on designs as I am able to visualize my imaginations.
